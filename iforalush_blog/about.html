About Me

Hello! I’m Ifra Khatoon, the creative soul behind Ifora Lush. I design clean, elegant, and functional websites with a personalized touch — all with the help of modern AI tools and artistic inspiration.

As a hijabi designer who values creativity, ethics, and accessibility, I make sure every layout I create is unique and impactful — whether it's for personal blogs, portfolios, or professional websites.

My mission is simple: “Design inspired by values, made for everyone.”
